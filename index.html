COGS 102C - Course at UCSD:
Cognitive Design

Cognitive Design is a course in the top-ranked cognitive science department at UCSD which trains students to think like a designer and perfect the process of ideating, prototyping, testing, getting feedback, and iterating.

After many trials and iterations, my team designed and prototyped a water tracking system that aims to improve water conservation on campus. This system informs students of their water usage and offers information on where and how they can conserve based off of their water habits.
